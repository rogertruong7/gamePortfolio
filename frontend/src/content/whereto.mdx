A social mobile app for sharing and discovering date plans and activity itineraries. Users create structured Plans with ordered steps linked to real places, execute them in real-time, and publish posts about their experiences for others to discover and follow.

---

## Overview

Whereto is a full-stack mobile application that combines itinerary planning with social sharing. Think of it as a social network for curated date ideas and activity plans. Users build step-by-step Plans (each step tied to a real location with notes and estimated duration), track their progress through a plan in real-time, then publish a Post about the experience — complete with images and captions — for their followers to see and save.

---

## Features

- **Plan Creation**: build structured itineraries with ordered steps, each linked to a place with notes and estimated duration
- **Plan Execution**: start a plan and track progress through each step in real-time, marking steps as complete as you go
- **Social Feed**: browse posts from people you follow on a personalised home feed
- **Explore Page**: discover new plans and posts from the broader community
- **Post Publishing**: turn completed plans into shareable posts with images and captions
- **Comments**: engage with posts through a comment system
- **Post Saving**: bookmark posts to revisit later
- **Follow System**: follow and unfollow other users to curate your feed
- **User Profiles**: view profiles with post history and follower/following counts
- **Tag System**: categorise plans and posts with tags for easier discovery
- **Authentication**: secure JWT-based auth powered by Amazon Cognito

---

## Architecture

The project follows a clean layered architecture on both the backend and mobile sides:

### Backend (Go)
- **Handlers**: HTTP request/response handling with input validation
- **Services**: business logic layer orchestrating operations
- **Repositories**: data access layer with GORM ORM queries
- **Models**: User, Plan, PlanStep, Post, Comment, Follow, Tag, PostSave, PlanExecution

### Mobile (React Native)
- **Expo Router**: file-based routing with tab navigation (Home, Explore, Create, Plans, Profile, Settings)
- **TanStack Query**: server state management with caching and background refetching
- **API Layer**: typed API client with auth token injection

## Functionality

### Plans
- Create plans with a title, description, and ordered list of steps
- Each step includes a place name, notes, and estimated duration
- Browse and manage your saved plans
- Execute a plan step-by-step in real-time

### Posts
- Publish a post from a completed plan with images and captions
- View posts on your home feed or the explore page
- Comment on posts
- Save/bookmark posts for later

### Social
- Follow and unfollow users
- View user profiles with their posts and stats
- Personalised feed based on who you follow

## Tech Stack

- **Mobile**: React Native (Expo), TypeScript, TanStack Query, Expo Router
- **Backend**: Go (Fiber framework), GORM ORM
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, Redis (caching)
- **Auth**: Amazon Cognito (JWT-based)
- **Storage**: Amazon S3 (images)
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, Docker Compose
